ABOUT

My name is Belden Granada, an artist from the San Francisco, Bay Area.

At 26, I like to explore themes reflective of an individual at this age; longing and dependency, and a loathing towards wealth. My inspiration stems from an interest in; music, writing, and people.

I always strive to tell a story, that takes people away, that makes them believe – even if just for a minute; I want to take people on a journey, that makes them want to fight and fall in love.

If I can do that, then I did my job.
