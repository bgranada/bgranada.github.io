DREAM HOUSE

Inspired by the Deafheaven song of the same name, DREAM HOUSE, explores themes of internal conflict, longing, dependency, and anger towards wealth. I envisioned this piece displayed in a medium sized gallery/space with 4 walls each being projected on.
